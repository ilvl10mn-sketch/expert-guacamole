Avocado smoothie

A silky, banana-free green smoothie that leans on avocado, berries, and greens for a clean, satisfying boost.

Prep 5 min
Serves 1–2 glasses
Mood Energizing

Ingredients

For one vibrant green smoothie

  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup kale, stems removed
  • 1 medium carrot, chopped
  • 1–2 tsp avocado oil (or ½ ripe avocado)
  • Splash orange juice
  • A few strawberries

Tip: Add water or unsweetened almond milk until you reach your ideal thickness.

Instructions

Blend in this order for the best texture

  1. Blend until smooth

    Blend on high until desired texture.

  2. Add leafy greens

    Add spinach, kale, and the chopped carrot on top of the liquid.

  3. Top with fruit & yogurt

    Add frozen fruit and Greek yogurt if using for extra creaminess.

  4. Finish with avocado oil & liquids

    Add avocado oil and liquids (water or almond milk) last, then blend on high until desired texture.

Why this recipe works

  • Balanced sweetness: Strawberries give natural sweetness and a smooth texture.
  • Better vitamin absorption: Avocado oil helps your body absorb fat-soluble vitamins A and K from the greens and carrot.
  • Nutrient-dense base: Carrot plus leafy greens deliver the nutrient punch you’re after in every sip.
  • Clean creaminess: Optional Greek yogurt makes it creamy and satisfying without turning it into a sugary fruit bomb.

Enjoy immediately while cold and bright green.
